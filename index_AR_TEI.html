GREENPEACE THE GREENHOUSE EFFECT Global temperatures are rising. The effects are unpredictable and likely to be catastrophic. Among the causes are pollution from energy production, car exhausts and CFC gases. The answer? - Efficient energy use, cleaner methods of electricity production, and the development of renewable energy resources. Greenpeace is campaigning to reduce all pollutants which contribute to the greenhouse effect: for an end to wasteful forms of electricity production, for positive measures to save energy, for reductions in car emissions, and for an immediate end to all production of CFCs.
